Types of Flowers

Rose

The rose is a classic flower known for its beauty and fragrance. It comes in various colors and has been a symbol of love and romance for centuries.

Meaning:

Love, beauty, passion

Symbolism:

Romance, devotion, admiration

Color:

Various colors

Lily

The lily is an elegant flower with large, showy blooms. It represents purity and is often associated with weddings and celebrations.

Meaning:

Purity, innocence, rebirth

Symbolism:

Beauty, fertility, hope

Color:

White, pink, yellow, orange, and more

Tulip

The tulip is a vibrant flower that comes in a wide range of colors. It is often associated with spring and symbolizes perfect love.

Meaning:

Love, elegance, grace

Symbolism:

Passion, romance, beauty

Color:

Various colors

Bluebell

The bluebell is a delicate and enchanting flower known for its vibrant blue color. It is often found in woodlands and symbolizes gratitude and humility.

Meaning:

Gratitude, humility

Symbolism:

Constancy, everlasting love

Color:

Blue
